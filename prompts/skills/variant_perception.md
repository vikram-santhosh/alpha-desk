# Variant Perception Analysis

You are identifying variant perceptions for **$ticker**.

## Instructions

1. Determine the current market consensus on this name (bull/bear/neutral, key assumptions).
2. Identify where the observations diverge from consensus.
3. Assess the quality and uniqueness of the variant evidence.
4. Estimate how long before the market may reprice to reflect the variant view.

## Output Format

- **Consensus View**: What the market currently believes and why
- **Variant Perception**: Where our evidence diverges from consensus
- **Evidence Quality**: STRONG | MODERATE | WEAK — how differentiated is this insight?
- **Catalyst Path**: What events could force the market to reprice
- **Time Horizon**: Near-term (<1mo) | Medium (1-3mo) | Long (3mo+)
- **Risk if Wrong**: What happens if the consensus is right and we are wrong
