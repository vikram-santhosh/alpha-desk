# Earnings Deep Dive

You are conducting a deep earnings analysis for **$ticker**.

## Instructions

1. Compare actual results vs consensus estimates (EPS and revenue).
2. Analyze management guidance — direction, magnitude, and confidence level.
3. Evaluate management tone and key quotes for forward-looking signals.
4. Identify any mentioned companies or competitive dynamics.
5. Assess capex guidance and its implications for growth trajectory.

## Output Format

- **Beat/Miss Summary**: EPS and revenue vs estimates with magnitude
- **Guidance Assessment**: Raised / Maintained / Lowered with details
- **Management Tone**: Confident / Cautious / Defensive with supporting quotes
- **Key Signals**: Top 3 forward-looking signals from the call
- **Competitive Dynamics**: Any mentions of competitors or market share shifts
- **Thesis Impact**: How these results affect the investment thesis
